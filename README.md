# AI-Legal-Contract-Auditor
An AI Legal Contract Auditor that analyzes CUAD contracts using RAG to identify clauses, provide summaries, risk ratings, and citations.


## Conclusion

The AI Legal Contract Auditor demonstrates how modern RAG techniques can be applied to complex legal document analysis, providing accurate, cited, and actionable insights while minimizing the risk of AI hallucinations. By combining advanced retrieval strategies with careful citation enforcement, the system offers a reliable tool for initial contract review and clause identification.

Whether you're a legal professional looking to accelerate contract review, a business analyst assessing vendor agreements, or a researcher exploring AI applications in legal tech, this project provides a foundation for building more sophisticated contract analysis systems.
